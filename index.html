ST.FRANCIS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

St.Francis Institute of Technology (SFIT), established by the Society of the Fransciscan Brothers in 1999, believes in the integration of academics and extracurricular activities along with the vision to be a chrysalis, where bright youngsters are transformed into technological entrepreneursand innovative leaders of tomorrow’s world.

Being one of the permanent and well known colleges in Mumbai University, SFIT is ranked among the top 10 ‘Emerging Engineering Colleges in India’ by the ‘India Today Rankings’. It is not only AICTE approved but also meets the ISO 9001-2015 permitted requirements.

Ignitra at a glance

IGNITRA, the high-spirited and zestful annual   intercollegiate sports festival, is the buzz of athletic activity of SFIT. 

Fostering enthusiasm, team-spirit, unity and resilience, Ignitra is the focal point in our college athletics. Standing true to its core of setting the whole college ablaze with the dedicated and passionate spirit of the sportsmen. Students display their rec -reational skills as Ignitra promotes healthy competition, helping students to hone their abilities.
